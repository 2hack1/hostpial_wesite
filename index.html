Our Facilities

Teaching Block

College has spacious lecture halls with comfortable seating arrangements. Lecture halls are equipped with current teaching technologies to make teaching learning activities more effective.

OBSTRETICAL AND GYNAECOLOGICAL

Maternal and child health is of prime attention in our country. In this lab students learns the mechanism of delivering baby, care of babies and mothers and to tackle emergencies through simulators.

PRE-CLINICAL SCIENCE LAB

Anatomical models, charts, slides and specimen are available for better understanding of human physiology. Equipments for biomedical tests are also available for practice.

NURSING FOUNDATION LAB

Nursing lab of college is equipped with all advance equipments,manikins and simulators to enhance the nursing skills of learners.

NUTRITION LAB

Equipped nutrition lab is available where learners learn to prepare and serve therapeutic diets for patients.

COMMUNITY HEALTH NURSING

Community lab is available to develop skills required to work within the rural and urban communities.

AUDIO-VISUAL LAB

LCD, OHP, VCD, TV, CDs, flash cards, posters, puppets, flannel graphs, flips charts and other teaching materials are available for easy understanding of subject.

COMPUTER LAB

The college has well established computer lab with internet facility which is accessible to all learners to keep themselves updated with the current trends and technologies in nursing.

LIBRARY

College has huge library with a wide collection of more than 5000 books, national and international nursing journals, periodicals, on- line journals, research thesis of graduate, post-graduateand doctoral programme to cater the intellectual needs of learners.

MULTI-PURPOSE HALL

Multipurpose hall with seating arrangement of 1000 is accoustically designed with modern amenities.

CONFERENCE ROOM

College has well furnished conference hall and seminar rooms with modern facilities of audio-visual aids, i.e. Smart Board, LCD, OHP,TV,VCD and microphone etc.

TRANSPORTATION

Bus facility is available for students and staff to make easy reach for college and hospital. 24 hours van facility is available for students in case of emergency& sickness.

STUDENT WELFARE

In sickness, students are given special care in student welfare room. College has Multi-Speciality hospital. College has van/ambulance to transport students to hospital in emergency.

Bsc Nursing

Our BSc Nursing program equips students with essential knowledge and hands-on experience to excel in healthcare. Graduates gain expertise in patient care, clinical procedures, and professional nursing practice.

The BSc Nursing course prepares students to become competent and compassionate nurses. It covers subjects such as anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, community health, and clinical practice, enabling graduates to provide holistic care in diverse healthcare settings.

GNM ( General Nursing Midwifery)

The GNM course equips students with knowledge in patient care, community health, maternal and child nursing, and practical hospital experience. Graduates are prepared to provide compassionate, professional, and ethical nursing care in diverse healthcare settings.

he GNM course prepares students in nursing fundamentals, midwifery, community health, and clinical practice. Graduates gain practical experience to deliver compassionate patient care across hospitals, clinics, and community settings, ensuring professional competency and holistic health support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our nursing programs, admission process, and student support.

What courses does your nursing college offer?

We offer BSc Nursing, GNM (General Nursing Midwifery), MSc Nursing, and various certificate programs combining theory and practical training.

How can I apply for admission?

Admissions can be applied online through our official portal. Ensure you meet eligibility criteria and submit required documents before the deadline.

Do you provide practical training in hospitals?

Yes, our students gain hands-on experience through partnerships with leading hospitals and community health centers, ensuring real-world clinical exposure.

Are there opportunities for career growth after graduation?

Graduates can pursue advanced degrees, specialize in nursing fields, or take leadership roles in hospitals, healthcare organizations, and research.

Does the college offer student support and counseling?

We provide academic guidance, mentorship, and counseling services to help students excel in studies and personal development.

How is the campus environment and facilities?

Our campus offers modern classrooms, fully-equipped labs, a library, hostels, and recreational facilities to support holistic student development.
